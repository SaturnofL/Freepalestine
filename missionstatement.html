Our Commitment

Free Palestine’s mission is to bring awareness to the Palestine vs Israel events that are currently going on in the world. We wish to inform the uninformed and create funding for the people of Palestine, because we’re all for helping those who are oppressed be free.

Our Vision

Palestine is a state in the country of Israel that has been oppressed since the war began in 1948. We want to help the people of Palestine because no one should be oppressed like this for decades on end.

Why We Exist

Our organization was founded with the belief that Palestine should be free from its oppressors, and to create funding so the people of Palestine have resources to help them in these trying times.
